Services

We have partnered with Audev BV, one of the oldest automobile software development companies in Europe, who provides a comprehensive Dealer Management System (DMS) - CarIT, that today powers some of the premium brands like Ford, Opel, Toyota, Volvo and more in the European markets.

CarIT the first open DMS, capable of handling multiple makes, manufacturers, suppliers and languages. Its leading edge technologies allow it to be fully integrated and interfaced with the communications tools (DCS) and applications programs (DFS) of the various manufacturers.
